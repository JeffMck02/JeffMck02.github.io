Project Overview

This project examines how cooking functions as a form of cultural transmission, memory, and identity. Through interviews with chefs and culinary practitioners, it documents how food traditions are preserved, adapted, and shared across generations.

Research Focus

  • How does cooking preserve cultural memory?
  • How is food knowledge transmitted through everyday practice?
