Interview Excerpts

Can you tell me how you first got started in cooking and what drew you to it in the first place? Are there any particular people or places that shaped how you understand food? Are there any dishes or ingredients that are particularly meaningful to you? Why? Do certain foods or smells bring you back to specific moments, memories or places in your life? Are there any dishes you feel are especially tied to your culinary identity or background? Are there any cooking traditions that you feel committed to maintaining? Do you find cooking as something that brings people together, in what ways? Do you ever find yourself having to adapt or change dishes because of ingredients, location or audience? Do you think these adaptations can change the meaning of a dish? Do you see cooking as a way of preserving something important to you or others? Are there any stories behind a dish you like to make that you want to be remembered?
